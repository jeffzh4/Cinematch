About

A film recommender for the overwhelmed.

CineMatch was built to solve a small but persistent frustration: that finding the right film to watch on any given night takes longer than the watching itself. Endless thumbnails, samey blurbs, the slow drift toward whatever's loudest on the homepage.

Instead, three questions. Tell it your mood, the kind of film you're after, and what the night looks like. It returns six picks, each with a brief, spoiler-free reason for being there. No infinite scroll, no thumbnails — just an answer.
